Our Vision
Trusted by Patients to Meet Their Needs
Our vision is to be one of the leading primary health providers in the area, expanding our services to reach additional community members. Our reputation is that we provide 100% access with 0% disparities in health care delivered. In order to accomplish this, we work to be trusted by patients, a valued partner in the community, and creators of positive change.

Our Center
A Valued Partner in the Community
We are a valued partner in the community as both a leader and a participant in addressing community health issues. We contribute to the community by being a leading health education center. We provide classes based on the most current information available. We are a model for other clinics with our innovations in pilot health care programs and clinic operations.

Our Mission
Creators of Positive Change
Our Mission is to provide quality health care with the belief that all individuals have the right to considerate service at all times with recognition of their personal dignity. We are a catalyst for positive change in the community, working with others to get things done for the good of the whole community dynamics needs. St. Francis is set to meet those needs.
